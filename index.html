Illustrated catalog of scorpions. Part II (2013).
Bothriuridae; Chaerilidae; Buthidae I., genera Compsobuthus, Hottentotta, Isometrus, Lychas, and Sassanidotus The volume has 400 pages in A4 format, is hard-bound, and contains 1621 color photos, 253 black-and-white photos, 110 drawings, and 26 distribution maps. Contains 332 species including 143 synonyms of which 19 are new (total of 475 taxa). Described are eight new species of the family Buthidae. The enclosed pdf of 14 selected pages give an idea about the internal arrangement and appearance. The book can be ordered directly from the first author at kovarik.scorpio@gmail.com. The price is 120 euros, which includes postage.

Scorpions of Cuba (2012).

The book can be ordered directly from the second author at kovarik.scorpio@gmail.com. The price is 60 euros, which includes postage.
